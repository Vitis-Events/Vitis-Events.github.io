GopherCon UK

GopherCon UK is a professional development and industry event for anyone interested in Google's Go programming language, held at The Brewery, in the heart of the City of London.

With over 500 delegates, speakers, and sponsors, GopherCon UK aims to deliver fantastic up-to-date content about Go programming and related technologies in a comfortable and professional setting.
